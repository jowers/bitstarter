Everyone loves fantasy football. How about combining it with the excitement of fantasy share trading?

Ever wondered if one Robin van Persie is more valuable than 10 Grant Holts? How many Ben Fosters give the same return as one Petr Cech? Now you can find out. In the footy-100 fantasy competition, instead of buying a football team, invest in shares of the footballers. Instead of points, get dividends. Then, when your team have earned you more funds, it's time to make the call. Do you invest further in your exitsing team or upgrade to a higher rated player? The choice is yours
